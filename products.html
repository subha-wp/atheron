Mobility · 2-wheel

Electric scooty packs

Compact, high-power modules for electric scooters — optimized for urban stop-and-go, fast acceleration, and reliable daily charging. Configurable voltage and capacity tiers for OEM and retrofit programs.

  • Integrated smart BMS with cell balancing & fault isolation
  • LFP / NMC chemistry options to match range & cost targets
  • IP-rated enclosures; vibration & shock validated
  • CAN / UART telemetry for vehicle controller integration

Commercial · 3-wheel

E-rickshaw batteries

Heavy-duty traction packs engineered for all-day passenger and cargo duty. Deep discharge tolerance, consistent power under load, and long calendar life for fleet operators who cannot afford downtime.

  • High continuous discharge for gradients & full passenger loads
  • Reinforced module structure for rough-road environments
  • Swappable or fixed-pack architectures (program dependent)
  • Remote diagnostics hooks for fleet management systems
48V–60V systems 5–10+ kWh range Deep-cycle optimized

Stationary · C&I · Utility

ESS battery systems

Rack and container-class energy storage for peak shaving, backup, renewable firming, and microgrids. Modular architecture scales from commercial sites to utility projects with consistent safety architecture end-to-end.

  • AC or DC coupled options; PCS integration support
  • Multi-hour duration configurations; scalable rack design
  • Advanced thermal design & fire-safety minded layouts
  • EMS-ready signals for SOC, SOH, and alarm streaming
