Our story

From ambition to industrial scale

Atheron was founded to close the gap between growing demand for electric mobility and stationary storage — and the need for battery supply that is consistent, traceable, and safe. We invested early in process discipline, supplier partnerships, and talent depth so customers can plan programs with confidence.

Today our roadmap spans high-volume packs for two- and three-wheel electric vehicles, fleet-oriented solutions, and ESS platforms for commercial and utility customers. Every line of business shares the same quality system backbone and the same expectation: deliver what we promise, on spec and on time.

  • Vertically aligned Electrode-to-pack capability under one roof
  • Customer programs OEM, fleet, and project-led engagements
  • Safety first Design reviews, testing gates, and field feedback loops
